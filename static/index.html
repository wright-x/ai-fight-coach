How to Film for the Best Results

📷 Angle: Position the camera at chest height, directly in front of you. Avoid high or low angles.
💡 Lighting: Ensure you are well-lit from the front. Avoid backlighting (e.g., standing in front of a bright window).
🖼️ Framing: Keep your entire body, from head to toe, within the frame throughout the entire video.
👤 Focus: The AI performs best with only one person in the video. Please ensure you are alone in the shot.
⏱️ Duration: Keep your video concise. The maximum upload duration is 30 seconds.
